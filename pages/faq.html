FAQS- Pet Portraits - Cartdove

  • Can I see my portrait before it ships?
  • Yes! Select "Preview Before Shipment"
  • Do you ship Worldwide?
  • Yes, we do!
  • How do you male portraits?
  • We have a team of highly skilled artists that use our propietary set of photo editing skills. We use special techinques combined with many digital brushes and blends. Each work of art is based on your pet's photo!
  • Do you only make portraits of dogs and cats?
  • All pets all welcome!
  • Can I have more than one pet in my portrait?
  • Yes! We can put up to 5 pets.
  • Are the photos from my phone's camera good enough?
  • If that's the only photo you have from your pet we can make it work
  • Can I change my order?
  • You can change your order up to 5 hours after you place it. Because each order is one of a kind after 5hours has passed, any changes will be at our discretion
